Classmates

Since October 2022 in Semester 1, my journey through N5CDIM1105A has been enriched by the incredible individuals I've had the privilege of studying alongside. These past few semesters have been filled with laughter, shared struggles, and unwavering support.

Each of my classmates holds a special place in my heart. I've witnessed their dedication, their unique talents, and their unwavering spirit. Whether it was late-night study sessions, group project triumphs, or simply sharing a meal together, these memories will forever be cherished.

Equine

It has been immensely enjoyable to join the Equine Unit at UiTM. It has fostered important life skills in addition to the apparent advantages of physical fitness. My interpersonal, leadership, and communication abilities were refined by overseeing programs, organizing volunteers, and managing uniforms. I found hidden hobbies and developed a strong network within the Equine world by stepping outside of my comfort zone. In addition to enriching my diploma path, these events have given me priceless memories and talents that I will surely use for the rest of my life.
