I Lived in Port Dickson

A lovely fusion of historical significance and natural beauty can be found at Port Dickson, a seaside jewel located in the state of Negeri Sembilan. It is a paradise for lovers of environment and water sports due to its immaculate beaches, glistening waterways, and abundant marine life. As someone who used to live in Port Dickson, I can still clearly remember the kind locals and the relaxed charm of the area. The town's rich past, which is reflected in its historic landmarks and colonial-era architecture, deepens its appeal. Port Dickson offers a memorable experience whether you're looking to unwind on the beach, explore historical sites, or indulge in regional cuisine.

Buku Fixi

Buku Fixi holds a special place in my heart as a avid reader. I deeply admire their commitment to championing new voices and pushing the boundaries of Malaysian literature. My collection of their books, featuring titles like 'Kougar' by Shaz Johar, 'Cekik' by Ridhwan Saidi, and 'Pecah' by Khairulnizam Bakeri, showcases the diverse range of compelling stories they publish. I appreciate how Buku Fixi has not only enriched the Malaysian literary landscape but also introduced me to a wealth of talented authors and captivating narratives.
